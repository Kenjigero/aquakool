About Our Product

In the Philippines, the production and sale of tumblers made from coconut waste is still in its early stages. However, there is a growing interest in environmentally friendly products, which will likely drive demand for the coconut shells and husk tumblers in the coming years.


Our business is founded on the concept of repurposing coconut waste to create a sustainable and environmentally friendly product. Our tumbler, AQUA KOOL has a handy compartment for keys and paper money, making it an ideal everyday companion.

MISSION

To create environmental-friendy and sustainable tumlbers out of coconut waste reducing the environmental impact of plastic pollution and supporting sustainable coconut farming practices
